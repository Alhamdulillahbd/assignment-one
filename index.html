Sundarbans: The Largest Mangrove Forest in Bangladesh

The Sundarbans is a vast forest in the coastal region of the Bay of Bengal

The Sundarbans is a vast forest in the coastal region of the Bay of Bengal which is one of the natural wonders of the world. Located in the delta region of Padma, Meghna and Brahmaputra river basins, this unique forest area extends across Khulna, Satkhira, Bagerhat, Patuakhali and Barguna districts.
